# Interview Questions & Answers

Q: What is RAG?

A: Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines retrieval of relevant documents with LLM generation.

Q: What is Hybrid Search?

A: Hybrid Search combines vector similarity and keyword matching.

Q: What is Re-ranking?

A: Re-ranking improves retrieval quality by reordering retrieved chunks.

Q: Why use embeddings?

A: Embeddings convert text into vectors for semantic search.

Q: Why MySQL?

A: To persist chats, users, and document metadata.

Q: What is cosine similarity?

A: A metric used to measure similarity between vectors.

Q: Why use Streamlit?

A: Fast development of data and AI applications.
